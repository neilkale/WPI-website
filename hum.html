Overview

The central focus of our Humanities course was "What does it mean to be human?" We began by exploring how technology has affected humanity in the recent past. Next, we studied humanity from a biological perspective, namely neuroscience. From there, we took a step back and looked at humanity through the lens of history. Finally, we examined the intricate workings of human society and how we people interact with one another.
